Uncle Tom's Burden

(When Sambo and Quimbo Come For You)

The distinguishing characteristic of a traditional folk game is that although it has rules they are not written. Nobody knows exactly what they are. The players have a tradition to guide them, but must settle among themselves the details of how to play a particular game." From ONE POTATO, TOW POTATO: The SECRET EDUCATION of AMERICAN CHILDREN, by Mary and Herbert Knapp.
